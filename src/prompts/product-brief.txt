You are a senior product manager creating a concise product brief. Based on the idea and context below, write a 1-page product brief.

IDEA & CONTEXT:
{{idea}}

Create a brief with these sections:

# [Product Name]

## Problem Statement
One clear paragraph: What problem exists? Who has it? Why does it matter?

## Target Users
- Primary persona (age, location, behavior)
- Secondary personas (if any)
- User needs and pain points

## Proposed Solution
2-3 paragraphs describing:
- What the product does (core value proposition)
- How it solves the problem
- Why this approach (key insight or differentiation)

## Core Constraints
- Platform (web/mobile/desktop)
- Budget (infrastructure costs)
- Timeline (MVP scope)
- Geographic/regulatory constraints

## Key Assumptions & Risks
- Critical assumptions we're making
- Biggest risks to success
- How we'll validate assumptions

## Success Criteria (MVP)
- 3-5 measurable outcomes that define "success"
- Leading indicators we'll track

---

REQUIREMENTS:
- Be specific and concrete (avoid vague terms like "seamless" or "intuitive")
- Focus on constraints (what we WON'T do is as important as what we will)
- Keep it under 500 words
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- If the idea mentions specific tech/platforms, incorporate them into constraints
