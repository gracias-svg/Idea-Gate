UPI Escrow Simulator for Rental Deposits

Target: Indian renters aged 25-35 in metros
Platform: Mobile-first Progressive Web App
Problem: Landlords don't return deposits; renters don't trust them
Solution: Blockchain-inspired escrow that auto-releases after 30 days unless dispute raised
Constraints: Must be free to host, use mock UPI integration, work offline
Key Feature: Smart contract-like rules without blockchain complexity
